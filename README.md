# de_wetter
This repo explores the weather condition in germany, mainly comparing between Munich (a city in Bavaria) and Berlin (the capital of Germany).

Munich situates in the South of Germany, which has a reputation of nice weather and warm temperature. In the meantime, people have been complaining about the Berlin weather.

However, how much worse is the weather in Berlin compared to Munich. In fact, does Berlin have worse weather at all? The truth might be surprising to you.

The analysis is limited to public data so the outcome of the comparison is dependent on public data accuracy.
